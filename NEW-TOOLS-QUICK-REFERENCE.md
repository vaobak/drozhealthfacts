## üîß How to Use Each Tool

### 1. Ideal Weight Calculator
**Input:**
- Height (cm or inches)
- Gender (Male/Female)
- Body Frame (Small/Medium/Large)

**Output:**
- Ideal weight range (min-max)
- Average ideal weight
- 4 different formula results (Devine, Robinson, Miller, Hamwi)

**Use Cases:**
- Setting weight loss/gain goals
- Understanding healthy weight range
- Comparing different calculation methods

---

### 2. Macro Calculator
**Input:**
- Weight (kg or lbs)
- Height (cm or inches)
- Age (years)
- Gender (Male/Female)
- Activity Level (5 options)
- Goal (Weight Loss/Maintain/Muscle Gain)

**Output:**
- Daily calorie target
- Protein (grams, calories, %)
- Carbs (grams, calories, %)
- Fats (grams, calories, %)
- Visual progress bars

**Use Cases:**
- Meal planning
- Flexible dieting (IIFYM)
- Weight management
- Muscle building nutrition

---

### 3. Protein Intake Calculator
**Input:**
- Body Weight (kg or lbs)
- Activity Level (5 options)
- Goal (Weight Loss/Maintain/Muscle Gain)

**Output:**
- Daily protein target (grams)
- Protein per kg body weight
- Calories from protein
- Meal distribution (4 meals)
- Food examples

**Use Cases:**
- Muscle building
- Weight loss planning
- Athletic performance
- General health maintenance
