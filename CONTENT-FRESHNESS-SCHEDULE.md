# Content Freshness Schedule - Dr. Oz Health Facts

## Purpose
Maintain content freshness to improve SEO rankings and provide up-to-date information to readers.

## Update Strategy

### Monthly Updates (Top 10 Articles)
Update the top 10 performing articles every month with:
- Latest statistics and data
- New research findings
- Updated recommendations
- Current year in title (e.g., "2025 Guide")
- Fresh examples and case studies

**Schedule**: 1st week of each month

### Quarterly Updates (Top 20 Articles)
Update top 20 articles every quarter with:
- Comprehensive content review
- Add new sections
- Update images
- Refresh meta descriptions
- Add new internal links

**Schedule**: January, April, July, October

### Annual Updates (All Articles)
Review all articles annually for:
- Accuracy and relevance
- Outdated information
- Broken links
- Schema markup updates
- SEO optimization

**Schedule**: January each year

## Best Practices

### When Updating Content
1. **Preserve URL**: Never change article slug
2. **Update Date**: Always update "last updated" date
3. **Notify Google**: Submit updated sitemap
4. **Track Changes**: Document what was updated
5. **Test Thoroughly**: Check all functionality

### What to Update
✅ Statistics and data
✅ Research findings
✅ Recommendations
✅ Examples and case studies
✅ Meta descriptions
✅ Internal links
✅ Images (if better ones available)

### What NOT to Change
❌ Article slug/URL
❌ Core topic/focus
❌ Existing internal links (unless broken)
❌ Schema type
❌ Category
